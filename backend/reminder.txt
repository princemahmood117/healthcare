
		    ----- project analysis algorithm -----

(1) total users (4): Super_Admin(SA), Admin, Doctor, Patient

-> super_admin/admin will be created by 'seeding'
-> doctor will be created by admin/SA; (only using email-password)
-> patient will be self registered using email-password/social_login
-> doctor's cant be login using social_login method

(2) Features: 

-> Speciality : cardiology, Urology, Gastroantology... will be created by admin/SA
-> Doctor: assign specialities on a doctor while creating.

Relation (speciality and doctor): Many to Many
--> a single doctor may have multiple specilities OR a single specility of multiple doctors.

--> if many to many relation present, make a 'bridge' of DOCTOR_SPECIALITY which will be one to many relation

(3) Schedule: created by admin/SA

--> hospital will have a fixed schedule. Doctors will maintain their own schedule based on Hospital's schedule.
--> Doctor's schedule will be created by doctor himself.

Relation (Hospital_Schedule and Doctor_Schedule): Many to Many
--> make a brige 'DoctorSchedule' (created by doctor himself)
--> one doctor may have multiple schedules to see patients OR in one fixed schedule there are multiple doctors available.
